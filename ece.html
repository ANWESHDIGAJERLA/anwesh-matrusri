DEPARMENT OF ELECTRONICS & COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

The Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) was established in the year 2011 with an intake of 60 and enhanced to 120 during the year 2012. In the span of eight years the Department has grown to a complement of 21 faculty members with specialized in VLSI, Embedded Systems, RF , Digital Systems, Signal Processing, Communication Engineering and Microprocessor applications. Most of the Faculty having Doctorate Degree and few faculty under the verge of Completion of Doctoral program. The broad spectrum of subjects in the area of ECE is fully represented. Well established Laboratories along with necessary licensed software’s are available. The Department secured 3rd rank of Osmania University in the year 2017. Texas Instruments, US supported “Innovations Lab” is established. Department has signed MOU’s with Industry’s to impart training to Faculty and students in advances in Technology.
